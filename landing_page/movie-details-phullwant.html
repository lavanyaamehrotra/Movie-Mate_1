About the movie


Phullwanti is a renowned dancer celebrated across India. She arrives in Pune for a performance at the Peshwa`s court. But her performance is overshadowed by a comment from the court scholar, Venkat Shastri. Determined to seek justice, Phullwanti challenges him, leading to a competition that explores the clash between art and intellect. As the story unfolds, the question remains: which side will prevail as the true victor?
